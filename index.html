Games Catalogue

Grand Theft Auto 5

This is one of the best games to ever release on console and is still be played by millions of people around the world a decade after release. For me the second most anticipated game when I was in secondary school. Rating: 10/10

The Last Of Us

For me, this is the greatest story mode game of all time. It has action, horror, thriller and a bit of comedy all mixed in together and the attatchment you feel to the characters are so strong. I played this game countless times and it is no suprise why there is a HBO show about this game. Rating: 10/10

God Of War

This game dives into the world of Norse mythology and full of combat. You fight mythical characters and creatures and travel through lands to complete objectives. I did not play the first 3 but this game did not require it as it referenced the past story lines Rating: 8/10

The Last Of Us Part II

Now this game for me is numero uno. When it was announced in 2016, all I could think about is this game due to the first one and god knows how many times I rewatched the announcement trailer. The gameplay and story is unmatched and I've yet to play anything better. Rating: 10/10

God Of War Ragnarok

This is the first story mode game I played on the Playstation 5 and playing on a 4k monitor was just on a whole different experience. The combat and some of the cut scenes felt surreal and the story was immersive. Rating: 9/10

Red Dead Redemption 2

This game had me on a chokehold for a longtime. Besides The Last Of Us, I've not been emotionally invested in a game antagonist like this one. The story was very long but it didn't feel that way and it's just comforting playing in a world set in 1800's America. Rating: 10/10

Uncharted 4

I didn't get a chance to play the first 3 Uncharted games but did watch some playthroughs on YouTube but the 4th game released on Playstation 4. Another action packed story and great combat. Rating 7/10
